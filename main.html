🏛️ Raigad Fort — Capital of the Maratha Empire

Raigad Fort was the capital of the Maratha king Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The fort is situated at 2,700 ft above sea level. The coronation of Shivaji Maharaj took place here on June 6, 1674.

The Maha Darwaja (Great Gate) is one of the most impressive gateways in Indian fort architecture, standing at 66 feet tall with massive double doors studded with iron spikes.
